# Game Insights

## Insight 1: Centralized Combat Zones

Kill heatmaps show high combat density near the center of Ambrose Valley.

Action:
Redistribute loot to encourage wider map usage.

Impact:
Improves player distribution and pacing.

---

## Insight 2: Bots Die Near Player Routes

Bots frequently die along major traffic paths.

Action:
Improve bot positioning and AI pathing.

Impact:
Improves combat realism.

---

## Insight 3: Storm Death Clusters at Map Edges

Storm deaths occur mostly near map edges.

Action:
Adjust storm timing or provide clearer zone indicators.

Impact:
Reduces frustration and improves match flow.
